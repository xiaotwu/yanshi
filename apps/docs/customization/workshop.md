# Workshop

The **Workshop** is where you customize agent teams and office scenes. It opens as a centered
floating window with tabs: Installed, Agent Editor, Office Editor, and Create / Export.

## Packs

A **Workshop pack** bundles an agent team and an office theme. You can:

- **Install** a pack (import a `.zip`),
- **Enable / Disable** installed packs, and
- **Export** your current team + office as a re-importable pack.

Packs are validated on import. Unsafe packs are rejected (`YANSHI_WORKSHOP_002`); a general import
failure reports `YANSHI_WORKSHOP_001`. There is no online marketplace — local packs only, stated
honestly.

## Editors

- [Agent Editor](/customization/agent-editor) — edit each agent's name, station, accent,
  personality, and prompt.
- [Office Editor](/customization/office-editor) — lay out the atelier visually.

## Worker visuals

The animated workers follow the [Worker Design](/customization/worker-design) system. Future
Workshop **appearance/motion packs** could override worker art and animations — that capability is
planned; the registry is ready for it and claims nothing it cannot do.
